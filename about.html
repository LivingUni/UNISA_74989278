ABOUT
US

Established in 2023 for a UNISA project we are striving to bring mathamatics back into focus for the youth of south africa

Our Mission

To teach the CAIE curriculum, For each student to participate in brain development activities such as music, art, coding, rubix cube and chess, To teach the ancient art of Japanese Soroban(Abacus ) Education and Anzan (Mental Maths) , To prepare students to a level of university entrance and, To tutor our students with love and empathy imparting the values of 1 Corinthians 13 vs 4-8: 4 Love is patient, love is kind. It does not envy, it does not boast, it is not proud. 5 It does not dishonor others, it is not self-seeking, it is not easily angered, it keeps no record of wrongs. 6 Love does not delight in evil but rejoices with the truth. 7 It always protects, always trusts, always hopes, always perseveres.
