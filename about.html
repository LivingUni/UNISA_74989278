ABOUT
US

Established in 2023 for a UNISA project we are striving to bring mathamatics back into focus for the youth of south africa

Our Mission

At A+Academy, our commitment is to provide quality education through the CAIE curriculum. We believe in fostering holistic brain development for each student by engaging them in diverse activities such as music, art, coding, Rubik's Cube, and chess. Additionally, we offer specialized instruction in the ancient art of Japanese Soroban (Abacus) Education and Anzan (Mental Maths). Our goal is to prepare students for successful university entrance and beyond, while providing compassionate tutoring and nurturing values of patience, kindness, respect, and perseverance.

Our Approach

At A+Academy, we employ a unique approach to education that incorporates the power of A+Students, a renowned Japanese method. Our sessions begin with brain gym exercises, integrating the left and right brain hemispheres. Through activities like flash cards and speed writing, we keep students actively engaged. The use of the Soroban, an ancient abacus, refines motor skills and helps students grasp fundamental math concepts. A+Students is a powerful brain development program that fosters concentration, confidence, intelligence, and a love for math. Join us on this transformative journey at A+Academy.
