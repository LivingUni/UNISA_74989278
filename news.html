About our competitions

PAMA national & Internationals

Competitions involving abacuses provide an exciting platform for individuals to showcase their mental calculation skills and engage in friendly competition. These competitions often attract participants from different age groups and backgrounds, fostering a spirit of challenge and camaraderie.

Abacus competitions not only provide a platform for participants to display their skills but also promote a sense of achievement, encourage continuous improvement, and foster a love for mathematics. They inspire participants to push their boundaries and unleash their full potential in mental calculation.
