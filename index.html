HOW THE ABACUS PROGRAM WORKS

Gamified Mathematics learning

Incorporating fun, interactive elements, math games transform education into an exciting adventure. Engage in challenges, puzzles, and quests that promote critical thinking and problem-solving.

Physical Mental Model

Tangible learning through physical models is a powerful tool that brings math concepts to life. By engaging in hands-on activities and manipulating objects, learners create a physical mental model of abstract mathematical ideas.

Competitive and nurturing model

By creating a healthy competitive environment, students are motivated to strive for excellence and surpass their own limits. Friendly competitions encourage students to push their boundaries, showcase their skills, and develop a growth mindset.
